🎮 Games

  • Snake: /frontend/games/snake/index.html, card hidden/returned for testing; menu link added.
  • Flappy Bird: /frontend/games/flappy/index.html, card and menu link.
  • Coins: /frontend/games/coins/index.html, timed coin collecting; slowed tick and less frequent traps.
  • Breakout: /frontend/games/breakout/index.html, bounces, score, lives; integrated into list and menu.
  • Minesweeper: /frontend/games/minesweeper/index.html, 10×10/15 mines; fixed first‑click mine placement, flood fill, timer and flags.

❤️ Likes & Auth

  • JWT‑based likes with MySQL storage: verified and guest likes, deduplication and migration tools.
  • Front‑end likes script enabled for 42 language search pages; improved button visibility and hover/contrast.
  • Dev utilities: debug/migration pages and analytics for game events.

🔍 SEO & Indexing

  • Expanded sitemaps for all languages, added sitemap-ru.xml, fixed canonical and hreflang.
  • IndexNow with changed‑only ping; improved titles and descriptions in all languages.
  • Added Twitter Cards & JSON‑LD (News/Games/Home) and x‑default on the homepage.

🌍 Localization & Content

  • Large zh‑CN updates (menu, games, news, tools, profile) + zh‑CN RSS.
  • EN parity and fixes: links/OG/canonical, translations for News/Games/Tools/Auth.
  • Limited visible languages in the menu to RU/EN/zh‑CN; others prepared for later.
  • Removed outdated partners from the menu.

📢 Ads

  • Mail.ru banners across key pages; 320×50 mobile anchor; in‑feed ads on promo.
  • Global auto‑collapse for ad blocks on no‑fill; single script and re‑push.

📱 UX & Accessibility

  • Centered articles/news, stable scroll and paddings; aligned Google CSE search bar.
  • Dedicated accessibility.css, reduced motion, high‑contrast, large fonts; unified settings across pages.
  • Mobile: swipes, pull‑to‑refresh, haptics, lazy‑loading images.

🌱 Eco Calculators

  • New “Ecology” category and product footprint calculator; expanded database and improved loading UX.

🧹 Misc

  • Front‑end structure cleanup (menu/main/scripts/styles → frontend/…), PWA and service worker fixes.
  • Promo geo‑filter and tracking pixel; removed obsolete labuba prototypes.

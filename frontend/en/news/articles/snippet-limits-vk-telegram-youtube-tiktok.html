Snippet Length Limits (VK / Telegram / YouTube / TikTok)

September 15, 2025
Goal: avoid truncation and maximize CTR. Below — a cheat‑sheet of recommended lengths and before/after examples.

1) Summary table

Platform Title (reco./limit) Description/Text (reco./limit) Preview/Truncation
VK ≤ 60–70 / — ≤ 140–160 / up to 4096 post chars Link preview trims title ~70, desc ~140+
Telegram ≤ 70 / — (from OG) ≤ 120–160 / 4096 message Preview from OG: title ~70, desc ~120–140
YouTube ≤ 60 / limit 100 ≤ 150–200 (above‑the‑fold) / limit 5000 2–3 lines visible initially
TikTok ≤ 60–80 / — ≤ 80–150 (visible) / up to ~2200 Caption collapses after ~80–150 chars

2) VK: before/after

BEFORE

“An extremely long headline about our new feature that will be truncated in every preview and reduce CTR…”

AFTER

“New feature: faster and simpler” — short and clear. Subheader: “Key benefits and examples” (≤ 140).

3) Telegram: before/after

BEFORE

“[New article] The complete UTM guide for all marketing channels with checklists and examples”

AFTER

“UTM: complete guide” — “GA4, templates for Google/Meta/VK, common mistakes”. Title ≤ 70, desc ≤ 120–140.

4) YouTube: before/after

BEFORE

“How we built SEO in 30 days: a full breakdown from audit to rollout, analytics and reporting [2025]” (105 chars)

AFTER

“SEO in 30 days: step‑by‑step plan” — “Audit, rollout, reporting. Pitfalls & tips (2025)”. Title ≤ 60, first 150–200 chars carry value and CTA.

5) TikTok: before/after

BEFORE

“Check our long explanation with lots of details and links — read till the end!”

AFTER

“3 UTM mistakes that waste traffic” — “Check source/medium, encoding and redirects. Full guide via link”.

6) Tips for previews & metadata

  • Set proper OG meta (title/description/image) for VK/Telegram previews.
  • Keep title ≤ 60–70, description ≤ 140–160 for cross‑platform safety.
  • Put value in the first 1–2 lines of description.
  • Validate previews before publishing (drafts, bots, share tools).

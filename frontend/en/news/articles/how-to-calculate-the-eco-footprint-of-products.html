üìä Why It's Important to Calculate Product Footprints

  • Reducing Climate Impact: The food industry is responsible for ~26% of global greenhouse gas emissions
  • Saving Water Resources: Up to 70% of fresh water is used for food production
  • Preserving Biodiversity: Agriculture is the main cause of deforestation
  • Personal Awareness: Understanding the connection between your choices and global processes

üí° Tips for Reducing Your Food Footprint

  • Reduce Meat Consumption: Especially red meat (beef and lamb)
  • Choose Local: Locally produced products have a smaller transportation footprint
  • Seasonality: Buy vegetables and fruits in their natural harvest season
  • Reduce Waste: Plan your purchases and use products completely
  • Organic vs. Conventional: Organic products may have a smaller footprint, but not always

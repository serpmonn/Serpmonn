Suggestion review process

1. Moderation — suggestion review within 1-2 days

2. Evaluation — analysis of complexity, usefulness and urgency

3. Planning — adding to development backlog

4. Implementation — best suggestions are implemented in upcoming updates

5. Feedback — if email is provided, status of the idea will be communicated

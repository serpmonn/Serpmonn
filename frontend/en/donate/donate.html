How to support the project?

  1. Choose a convenient method from the options below

All payments are processed through secure payment systems of selected services.

Subscription / One-time payment

Boosty

  • Click the button on the right to go to Boosty
  • Click the donate button
  • Available: SBP, bank cards, PayPal
  • Monthly payments can be set up

    💡 Useful Information

    Depreciation depends on mileage, time in service and usage conditions
    Commercial use increases wear by 1.5-2 times
    Regular maintenance and quality service slow down depreciation
    Climate conditions affect body and electronics wear
    Hybrid and electric vehicles have less engine depreciation, but the battery wears out from mileage
    Market factors: Consider price growth for new cars and demand for your model
    Regional features: Prices can vary greatly in different cities
    Inflation: Consider annual inflation to adjust initial cost

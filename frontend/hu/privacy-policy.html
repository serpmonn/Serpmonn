Privacy Policy

Introduction

The serpmonn.ru website values user privacy and takes all necessary measures to protect personal data. This privacy policy describes how and for what purposes user data is collected, used, stored and protected. By using this website, the user agrees to the terms set out in this policy.

1. Data collected

The website may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal data: name, email address, phone number.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, information about site visits (including pages visited, date and time of visit).
  • Data provided by the user: information provided when filling out forms on the site, including comments, reviews, requests and other feedback.

2. Use of data

Data is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve services.
  • To process requests and feedback.
  • For analysis and research to improve the site’s performance and ensure security.
  • To protect against unauthorized access and prevent attacks on the site.
  • To fulfill obligations to users.

3. Data storage

Data is stored for as long as necessary to achieve the stated purposes:

  • Personal data (name, email, phone) is stored until its deletion is requested or the use of services is terminated.
  • Technical data (IP addresses, browser data) is stored for no more than 30 days.

4. Transfer of data to third parties

Users’ personal data is not transferred to third parties without their consent, except when required by law:

  • Compliance with legal obligations, including requests from government authorities or court orders.
  • Fulfillment of the agreement with the user, including the provision of services or payments.

Transfer of data to third parties is carried out to the extent necessary to achieve the stated purposes.

5. Data protection

Measures are taken to protect data from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Personal data is protected using modern encryption technologies and other security measures. However, despite the measures taken, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. User rights

The user has the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data stored.
  • Request correction or deletion of data.
  • Object to data processing in certain cases.

7. Contact information

For questions related to the privacy policy, data processing or the exercise of rights, please contact: privacy@serpmonn.ru.
Terms of use and disclaimer are available in the “Disclaimer” section.

8. Changes to the privacy policy

All changes to the privacy policy are published on this page. We recommend checking back regularly for updates.
